As planned, I retreated to the [AQ Cabin](https://aqworks.com/yatsugatake) after 10 days of non-stop activity in Singapore, to rest and read, while catching up on work and life admin. My hiking ambitions for the Yatsugatake Range will have to wait until next year - right now, it's more the mood of hammocks, fireplaces and long walks. 

Japanese bush warblers *(uguisu)* and woodpeckers *(kitsutsuki)* fly through our front yard, visiting the fruit tree saplings we planted this spring. It's delightful to see them flitting about, to be able to put a "face" to the sounds. 

So grateful for the downtime.
