I worked out of my Tokyo office for the better part of the week. 

It was nice and peaceful. Tokyo in May has early summer weather, with cool mornings and evenings. I enjoyed meals and spontaneous conversations with my colleagues, and working to the sound of our neighboring middle school practicing for Sports Day. 

I also visited my sister and baby niece, who's growing into a spunky kid, and then did a big shopping spree before taking an early flight out to Singapore on Saturday. It's my first time back since May 2024, when I participated in the "[System and Self](https://and.sg/what-we-do/training-programmes)" course. 

The adventure continues.
