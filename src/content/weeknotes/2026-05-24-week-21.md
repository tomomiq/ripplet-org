I holed up in hotel conference rooms by day, and huddled with colleagues at hawker centers and bars at night, for the fourth of five modules of my "Gestalt in OD and leadership" training. 

Haven't processed it, so I won't attempt to write much here, but it was a fabulous week. I then had 2.5 days for a marathon of coffees, lunches and dinners with friends. Strangely not *that* tired, and not sick, thanks to a timely injection of propolis spray and all kinds of things that friends supplied me with. All is good.
