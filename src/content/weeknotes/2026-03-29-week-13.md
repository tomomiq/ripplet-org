Now that I’m tinkering and publishing on a near-daily basis on my [Build site](build.ripplet.org), Weeknotes has tumbled down the list of things I pay attention to — the new container is custom-built for how I want to think, and therefore it’s way more fun there. 

As tempting as it is, I’ve decided it’s not a good idea to fully migrate ripplet.org from Squarespace to Astro. The technical workload is more than I want to be investing, and more importantly, I’d be tempted to re-design everything. Let’s not go there. 

But good news, I learned it’s possible to switch over small pieces of it. I could re-build just the Weeknotes blog with the **same styles and URL structure**, and be free to extend it with dynamic elements and structured templates. This would be great —a smaller scope to work with, and future-forward in the sense that it would breathe new life into the weekly publishing habit.
