**Monday**: Movie night with French and Singapore friends. I was initiated to *La Cité de la peur*. 

**Tuesday**: Relaxed work day from Yatsugatake 

**Wednesday**: Sent out a [Postcard](https://buttondown.com/tomomiq/archive/a-postcard-from-pre-typhoon-yatsugatake/) (yay!) and travelled back to Tokyo. Pretty tough, with a cancelled train due to typhoon, and a delayed train because it hit a deer... 

**Thursday**: Normal work day, with a nap and 10pm call. 

**Friday**: Another normal work day, then drinks with friends. 

**Saturday**: Left Tokyo at 6:30 in the morning to spend the weekend with a friend who had moved outside of Kyoto. I got to explore the new neighborhood, and then we drove up the coast to Tsuruga. 

**Sunday**: Continued the drive up Echizen to Kanazawa, including a stop in Sabae, and hopped on the *shinkansen* home. 


I wanted to do all these things but it was a bit fragmented, and in hindsight, too much logistics to enjoy properly.
