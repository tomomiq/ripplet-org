_At a hotel bar in Frankfurt, Germany._

It’s been an incredibly long week, and I’m dumbstruck by how flattened I feel by the sheer volume of work that I put in. But it’s been a great lesson on how critical it is to be able to enjoy time spent together as a team, as a rock-solid foundation of any effort we put in. And there are the moments spent together on actual work, and the moment spent in between - the breaks, the lunches, the chit chat on our chat apps, and that last drink where we _don’t_ talk about work.
