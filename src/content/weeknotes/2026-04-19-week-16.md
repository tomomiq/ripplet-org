I finished the migration of this website and cancelled the Squarespace plan. It's a big deal to me, since this is a site I've had since I was a college kid. After the beloved Wordpress era, there was a practical Squarespace era, and now I'm on Astro, editing markdown and writing little scripts with the help of Claude Code. 

I figure it's not particularly interesting news to anyone else, as 95% of the changes are under the hood, but it was a nice surprise to get a lot of positive reactions on LinkedIn. 

Filled out the [project page](https://build.ripplet.org/projects/ripplet-org) on the Build.ripplet.org site, too, so now I feel complete.
