Not every book is working material. Some books are simply for pleasure.

This was one of those: a rock-and-roll thriller that a few of us read (devoured!?) around the same time. It moved quickly through our small circle.

.... which ended up sparking a conversation about what UX research can learn from investigative journalism. So there was that!

This book has an English translation, which I understand is v good. I was at London Gatwick airport last week and saw a window display for BUTTER and HOOKED, Yuzuki's 2015 novel which was also translated recently. Pretty cool.
